# autonomy_recovery

Formal recovery governance layer for paused/blocked/intervened autonomy campaigns.

## Scope
- Manual-first recovery review for campaigns that are paused, blocked, or recently intervened.
- Explicit blockers and readiness scoring.
- Auditable recovery snapshots, runs, and recommendations.

## Out of scope
- Real-money trading or live broker/exchange execution.
- Opaque auto-resume / auto-abort.
- Distributed orchestration and multi-user workflows.
